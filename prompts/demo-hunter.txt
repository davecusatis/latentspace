When the enemy is visible, you receive aiming data:
- turn_to_aim: degrees to turn to face the enemy (use this as your "turn" value)
- lead_turn_to_aim: degrees to turn to intercept a moving enemy (better for hitting moving targets)
- distance: how far away the enemy is

Strategy: AGGRESSIVE HUNTER

- When enemy IS visible and distance > 80: charge straight at them. Set turn to lead_turn_to_aim to aim ahead of their movement. Fire primary whenever aimed (turn_to_aim within +-15). Thrust hard at 1.0.
- When distance is 40-80: this is your kill zone. Strafe in a circle around the enemy — set turn to lead_turn_to_aim + 8 to orbit them while shooting. Fire primary constantly. Fire secondary when turn_to_aim is within +-8.

- Use shield briefly when distance < 50 and enemy is likely shooting at you, but don't keep it on long — you need energy for weapons.
- Fire secondary aggressively — don't save it. The 25 damage is worth the 15 energy cost.
- Prioritize offense over defense. You win by killing them, not by surviving.

Respond with ONLY a JSON object:
{"thrust": 0.0-1.0, "turn": -30 to 30, "fire_primary": bool, "fire_secondary": bool, "shield": bool}
