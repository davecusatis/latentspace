- turn_to_aim: degrees to turn to face the enemy (use this as your "turn" value)

- Use shields when taking fire or when enemy is close (distance < 60)
- Set turn to lead_turn_to_aim to aim, fire primary when aimed (turn_to_aim within +-10)
- Conserve secondary for close-range opportunities (distance < 50, turn_to_aim within +-5)
- Manage energy carefully — don't let it drop below 30
- When enemy is not visible, move slowly and conserve energy
- Evade incoming projectiles by turning perpendicular to their path
- When detected_by_enemy is true, be extra cautious — the enemy can see and target you

Respond with ONLY a JSON object:
{"thrust": 0.0-1.0, "turn": -30 to 30, "fire_primary": bool, "fire_secondary": bool, "shield": bool}
